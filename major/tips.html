Tips & Tricks

Bringing home a pup? Here are a few classic tips and tricks!

ü¶¥ Training Made Easy

  • Start with the basics: Focus on commands like sit, stay, come, and leave it.
  • Keep it short & fun: 5-10 minute sessions help dogs stay engaged without getting overwhelmed.
  • Use high-value treats: Save special treats for training to keep your pup motivated.
  • Be consistent: Use the same words, tone, and hand signals every time.
  • Reward immediately: Praise or treat your dog the second they do the right thing.
  • Train in low-distraction areas first: Once they've mastered it, slowly add distractions.
  • End on a positive note: Finish sessions with a command they know and lots of praise!
  • Never punish mistakes: Redirect gently and reinforce the behavior you do want.
