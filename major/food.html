Favourite Products

As a professional aesthetician, I'm always on the lookout for high-quality products that deliver real results. Here are some of my personal favorites that I use in my treatments and recommend to my clients for their at-home skincare routines.
Hale Derma Colorescience Skin Care

Hale Derma

Hale Derma is a Canadian skincare brand that offers a range of products for various skin concerns. Their products are formulated with high-quality ingredients and are suitable for all skin types. I particularly love their gentle yet effective approach to skincare that focuses on maintaining the skin's natural barrier.
Glymed Cleanser Skin Care

Glymed Mega Cream Cleanser

GlyMed Mega Cream Cleanser is a gentle, ultra-hydrating cleanser designed for dry and sensitive skin. It removes impurities while nourishing with rich emollients, leaving skin soft, smooth, and refreshed without stripping natural moisture. This is my go-to recommendation for clients with dehydrated or sensitive skin.
Colorescience Sun Protection Skin Care

Colorescience Tinted SPF

Colorescience Tinted SPF is a lightweight, mineral-based sunscreen that provides broad-spectrum protection while evening out skin tone with a natural tint. It's perfect for daily wear and offers protection against UV rays, blue light, pollution, and infrared radiation. The best part? It doesn't leave a white cast and feels amazing on the skin.
Colorescience Eye Care

Total Eye Renewal Therapy SPF

Total Eye Renewal Therapy SPF is a multitasking eye treatment that brightens, hydrates, and protects with SPF while reducing the appearance of dark circles and fine lines. The cooling metal applicator feels amazing and helps reduce puffiness. This is one of my personal must-haves and clients always notice a difference when they start using it.
Colorescience Lip & Cheek Skin Care

Colour Balms SPF

Colorescience Colour Balms SPF are multitasking, mineral-based balms that provide sun protection, hydration, and a hint of buildable color for lips and cheeks. These are perfect for adding a natural flush of color while still protecting your skin. I love recommending these for clients who want a simple, on-the-go product that does it all.
