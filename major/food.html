Favourite Products

As a professional aesthetician, I'm always on the lookout for high-quality products that deliver real results. Here are some of my personal favorites that I use in my treatments and recommend to my clients for their at-home skincare routines.
Meat Cooked

Lean Meats

Chicken, Turkey, Beef, is healthy and beneficial in moderation. Make sure to NOT provide any seasoning and cook the meat thoroughly!
Vegetable Dental Health Crunch

Carrots

Carrots pack tons of nutrients and is healthy for your dog to eat. It helps with their dental health and is mentally stimulating, since it provides a nice crunch.
Fruit

Blueberries

Blueberries are delicious and nutritious, packed in antioxidants to keep your pup healthy.
Processed

Peanut Butter

MAKE SURE TO BUY THE XYLITOL-FREE VERSION. Perfect in a toy or a treat. Easy to spread, and a dog-favorite.
Vegetable Cooked

Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are easy to eat, soft, nutritious, and filling for your pup.

Toxic Foods

These can cause anything from mild stomach upset to serious health issues:

Theobromine

Chocolate

Contains theobromine, toxic to dogs
Kidney Failure

Grapes and Raisins

Can cause kidney failure
Red blood cells

Onions and Garlic

Damages red blood cells
Persin

Avocadoes

Contains persin, which can be harmful
Xylitol

Xylitol

Extremely toxic to dogs.
Alcohol

Alcohol

Even small amounts can be extremely dangerous.
Cooked

Cooked bones

Can splinter and cause internal injuries
Nuts

Macadamia nuts

Can cause weakness, tremors, vomiting
