My Story

As a newcomer to the salon scene, I bring fresh ideas and a commitment to excellence. I studied at Elegance Schools in London, Ontario. I started out doing nails and have recently expanded to facials and spray tans. I am thrilled to be apprenticing as a skin therapist with Erika at NEWWay Aesthetics.

My Passion

Nails, nails, nails! You nailed it - my passion is your nails. I love coming up with new designs for your fingertips! But wait... expanding into facials and helping clients achieve the results they are looking for with their skin has made me even more excited about the field of medical aesthetics. I think I am going to love giving you healthy, glowing skin just as much as I love making your nails pop!
